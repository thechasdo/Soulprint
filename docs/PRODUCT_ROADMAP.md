# Soulprint Product Roadmap

## Launch with 3 tiers

1. Memory Seed — Free
2. Family Legacy — $8.99/mo or $86.30/yr
3. Forever Archive — $19.99/mo or $191.90/yr

Yearly pricing saves 20%.

## Scale to 5 tiers later

Do not launch with five tiers. Add these once users prove demand:

4. Estate Vault — after legal review / partner launch
5. Professional — funeral homes, family offices, churches, historical groups

## Premium add-ons

- Extra storage
- AI Story Builder
- Memorial QR Kit
- Printed Memory Book
- White-glove Setup
- Family Archive Migration
- Estate Vault

## Genealogy strategy

1. Build internal family tree.
2. Add GEDCOM import/export.
3. Add external links for Ancestry, FamilySearch, MyHeritage.
4. Pursue partner/API access later.
5. Never scrape external genealogy sites.
