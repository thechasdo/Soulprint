# Soulprint Brand System

## Core identity

Soulprint is not a generic Soulprint. It is a premium family legacy platform.

Brand words:

- Visionary
- Meaningful
- Timeless

Tagline:

> Memories Live On

## Brand feel

- Warm
- Secure
- Premium
- Family-centered
- Emotional without feeling gloomy
- Powerful without feeling cold

## Logo rule

The Soulprint logo must appear anywhere the word Soulprint is presented as a product identity:

- Header
- Footer
- Auth screens
- Dashboard
- Public Soulprint pages
- Metadata/favicon
- Share previews
- Printed QR kits
- Upgrade/pricing screens

## Colors

- Deep Navy: `#102A43`
- Seafoam Blue: `#58C7D9`
- Warm Sunset Pink: `#FF6F91`
- Warm Sunset Orange: `#FF9A4D`
- Soft Cream: `#FFF9F0`

## Product promise

Soulprint preserves the stories, voices, photos, videos, documents, family history, and legacy instructions that make a life unforgettable.
